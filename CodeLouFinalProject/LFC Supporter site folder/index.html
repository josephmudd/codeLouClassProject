WHO WE ARE

We are an actual registered Official Supporter Club for Liverpool Football Club. We have a wonderful group of people that eat, sleep, dream and love our team! During the Premier League season, we meet at Molly Malone's in the Highlands to watch matches, socialize, grab a pint, or few, and passionately cheer on our Reds! We would love it if you joined us...and we're sure you would too.
